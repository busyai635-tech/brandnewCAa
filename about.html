About Us

Lifeworks' approach is simple: We treat your finances like our own. We are crafted, not cobbled, for a delightful and precise financial experience defined by trust, integrity, and absolute compliance.

Our Mission: Empowering Businesses to Grow with Financial Clarity

We believe not just in balancing books, but in balancing growth with stability. Our mission is to provide businesses with the tax optimization and audit precision they need to scale confidently.

By combining traditional accounting wisdom with modern financial strategies, we align the success of your business with the integrity of our practice. Win-win!

Our Story

In 2010, fellow Chartered Accountants James Anderson and Sarah Jenkins noticed a major shift in the way businesses handled compliance. Founders didn't want to be interrupted by complex jargon; they wanted helpful, actionable financial intelligence.

Along the way, Lifeworks expanded beyond simple tax filing into a crafted, holistic firm offering audit, advisory, and wealth management.

Expertly led by our partners, Lifeworks uses its deep industry knowledge to help hundreds of scaling organizations grow better every day.
